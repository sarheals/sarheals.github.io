Day 666

Shot on Iphone, Edited in adobe premiere, 2022

Inspired by the Twilight Zone, I aimed to convey the uneasy feeling of being trapped in the same day over and over and over again. I shot the film in my apartment with my IPhone7. I created the music using my electric guitar and editing on adobe Premiere. .

Six Shots of Espresso Later

Paint on cardboard, Photoshop, 2022

This piece featuring a skull with bleeding eyes was created when I felt down. The process let me express my emotions by experimenting with the ink falling down the page. Later, I took a photo of the painting and put it in Photoshop, where I manipulated it by playing with the layering. The title was inspired by the people who come into the coffee shop I work and order scary amounts of espresso.

Zipper Head

Paper, fabric, glitter on cardboard, 2022

One day I was walking on the street and I stumbled upon some fashion magazines. I immediately knew I would use them for a collage. I recently got into the punk rock scene and those aesthetics and I took inspiration from that. I am also influenced by the DADA artist, Hannah Hoch, who often made collages with people with exaggerated features.
